# Help

## Orders & shipping

After checkout you will receive a confirmation email when payment completes. Standard orders ship according to the ETA shown in your bag. **Click & collect** orders are picked up at the location you selected; you will see **Ready for pickup** on your order when the store has prepared it.

## Returns

Contact the store using the email or phone on your order confirmation for return instructions.

## Customization

Use **Customize** on supported products to preview options before adding to your bag. Saved designs can be merged into your account when you sign in.
