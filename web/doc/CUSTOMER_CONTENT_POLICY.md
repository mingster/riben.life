# Customer-facing content policy (riben.life)

**Status:** Active
**Related:** [LUXURY_STOREFRONT_WORK_PLAN.md](./LUXURY_STOREFRONT_WORK_PLAN.md)

## Brand and assets

- Use **riben.life** naming, logo, and **owned** photography/illustration on all **customer** routes (`/`, `/shop`, `/customized`, `/account`, marketing pages in `(root)`, etc.).
- Do **not** use third-party **trademarks**, competitor **logos**, or **trade dress** meant to evoke another luxury house. Reference competitors for **UX patterns** only (see work plan), never for copy-paste visuals or wording.

## Product and pattern

- The **riben.life pattern** and product renders belong to the project; keep **asset paths** and alt text consistent with the real SKU (see `AGENTS.md` / `asset/pattern.jpg`).

## Review

- Before launch, run a **quick audit** of hero, PLP, PDP, and email templates for accidental stock art or brand collisions.
